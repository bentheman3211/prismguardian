NSFW Image Detection

Our hybrid approach combines local and API-based scanning for maximum accuracy and speed. Free tier uses NSFWJS, a lightweight local model that analyzes images in real-time without external dependencies. Pro tier upgrades to Google Vision AI, our most powerful API-driven scanner for enterprise-grade detection. We focus exclusively on channel media to keep the bot lightning-fast; avatars and user profiles are never scanned.

Raid Protection

When multiple accounts join and spam simultaneously, PrismGuard identifies the raid pattern instantly using behavioral analysis. Our intelligent system targets and removes raider accounts while keeping normal members completely safe and unaffected. The server locks down automatically to prevent further damage, purges malicious messages, and then safely re-opens once the threat is neutralized.
